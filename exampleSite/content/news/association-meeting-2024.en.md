## Building Consensus, Shaping the Future

The Chinese AI Application Development Association (CAIADA) successfully held its 2024 Annual Board Meeting on December 20, 2024. This meeting brought together leaders from academia, industrial R&D, and policy research to review the achievements of the past year and meticulously plan key objectives for 2025.

### Core Meeting Topics

1.  **Establishment of AI Ethics Framework**: An agreement was reached to release the first "AI Application Ethics Guidelines" for the Asia-Pacific region in Q1 2025, aiming for a balance between innovation and social responsibility.
2.  **Promoting Industry Standardization**: The board approved the standard-setting process for "Generative AI Safety Assessment," aimed at providing clear compliance paths for startups.
3.  **Deepening Global Collaboration**: Announced strategic partnerships with several international technology organizations to facilitate cross-border exchange of technical talent.

The Chairman stated: "The future of AI lies not only in breakthroughs in computing power but in how responsibly we guide its application. This meeting laid a solid foundation for the association's future."
