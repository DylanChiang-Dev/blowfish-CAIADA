## Igniting Inspiration, Driving Change

To discover the most promising AI technical solutions, the Chinese AI Application Development Association announces the official launch of the 2025 Global AI Innovation Contest (GAIC 2025). This is not just a competition but an incubation platform to bring cutting-edge AI ideas to life.

### Tracks and Rewards

*   **Smart Healthcare**: Solving healthcare resource allocation for an aging society.
*   **Green Energy Enhancement**: Optimizing carbon emission management and renewable energy efficiency using AI.
*   **Inclusive EdTech**: Breaking geographical barriers to achieve precise access to high-quality educational resources.

**Contest Highlights:**
- **Million Dollar Fund**: Finalists will have the opportunity to secure significant seed round investment.
- **Top-tier Mentors**: Experts from companies like Google and OpenAI will provide one-on-one technical guidance.
- **Global Stage**: Outstanding projects will be showcased at next year's Global AI Summit.

We invite innovators from all over the world to join us in painting a better future with AI.
